# 🛍️ Marketplace

A modern food & Shopping platform that connects customers with local businesses

## ✨ Features

- **Browse & Discover** — Explore restaurants, food stalls, and menus with ratings and reviews
- **Order Management** — Place orders seamlessly with a smooth checkout experience
- **Delivery Service** — Get food delivered right to your door
- **Real-Time Tracking** — Track your order and delivery live on the map
- **Dine-In** — Reserve a table and enjoy dining in at your favorite spots
- **Ratings & Reviews** — Rate restaurants, stalls, and individual dishes
